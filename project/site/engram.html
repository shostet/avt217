Story and Gameplay

Players control Max, a college student depicted in monochrome, as he attempts to regain his lost emotions by exploring past memories. Each level has a corresponding emotion, memory, and color. Strong core memories sit at the heart of each level protected by puzzles and challenges. By collecting these memories, Max is able to bring color back into his monochromatic world.

Thoughts

This was one of my first games created in a group setting, and as such, we ended up being a bit ambitious despite the time constraints. We mainly wanted to focus on visual storytelling, sticking to an abstract and dream-like setting. I created three musical themes for the game, and although this was also one of my first times composing, I think that I encapsulated the themes of the game well in these tracks.
