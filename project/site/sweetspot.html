Story and Gameplay:

Sweet Spot is an educational PSA game about diabetes inspired by my partner in the group who has Type 1 Diabetes. Players control an unnamed, diabetic college student trying to balance school stress, a social life, and a healthy diet. Players choose between different meals multiple times during a seven day week, careful to not let insulin levels get too high or low. Although not including in the demo, early concepts of the game included stress and happiness levels that players would also have to balance.
