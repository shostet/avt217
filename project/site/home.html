Welcome to my website!

Thank you for visiting my website! My name is Stephanie Hostetter, and I am sophomore Computer Game Design Major at George Mason University. I love many aspects of video games, especially story and music, but I'm still experimenting with what aspects of Game Design that I want to pursue in the future. While my all time favorite types of games are story-driven or JPRGs, I've also recently gotten into roguelikes and platformers as well. Please enjoy your stay!
