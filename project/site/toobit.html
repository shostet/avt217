Story and Gameplay:

Jellies, skellies, armies, and other enemies patrol a newly opened dungeon just for adventures. Unfortunately for one hardworking jelly, their life has changed forever when they accidentally fuse with a magical, orange gem, becoming a sentient being named Toobit. Players control this new amalgamation as they attempt to escape the dungeon, much to the dismay of the dungeonâ€™s master. As a squishy jelly, Toobit travels by jumping places. Toobit is also able to shift into gem mode, allowing them to slam into enemies and deal damage.

Thoughts:

One of the members in our group came up with the main premise, which we loved immediately. It was a lot of fun discussing world building, and I wish we had more time to develop all of our ideas. Definitely another project I would love to develop more in the future, especially since a lot of the sound assets were unable to be implemented.
