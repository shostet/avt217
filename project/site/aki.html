Story and Gameplay

Players control Aki, a loveable dog whose owner Remy may or may not have unleashed a swarm of hungry candies upon a nearby town (he’s still learning the basics of cooking, give him a break). The basic mechanics include stunning opponents on screen, making them vulnerable so that Aki can attack (and eat) them. While not in the demo version of the game, Aki’s speed, attack, and size varies depending on how many enemies she eats.

Thoughts

Adventures of Aki is a 2D platformer game demo that I created for a game design midterm. While I greatly enjoyed working on the game, Construct 3 limited what I could add, as well as the midterm deadline. I would love to recreate this game in a more versatile game engine such as Unity one day.
